Team SouL

The Indian pride

About the team About the players Tournament Stats

Team SouL is an esports organization based on Mumbai, India. The team is known for its magnificent performances in national and international tournaments of PUBGM The team was formed on December 2018. Its first professional tournament was DreamHack 2019. Their initial roster was MortaL(Captain), who is also the owner of this team, VipeR(support fragger), Owais(Entry fraggger)

Image:Team SouL during PMIS 2019

and RonaK(Sniper). They performed well in the tournament. Then the golden era of the team started. They registered for PUBGM India Series 2019 and played exceptionally well to advance their way to finals. In the grand finals, the team won the very first game. After performing exceptionally well in rest of the matches, SouL won the tournament with the award of the Squad with most kills. Then the team registered for PMCO 2019 Spring Split and after a tough competition with arch rivals, Team IND, SouL won this tournament also with nail biting last match. SouL then advanced its way to Global finals where SouL secured #12 spot out of 16 teams and also won a chicken dinner. SouL was then invited for the PUBGM star challenge and played alongside The RawKnee where the team finished #13. Then MortaL decided to take a break from competitive and focus on his YT. Scout replaced MortaL and after some internal conflicts, SouL broke as Owais, RonaK and scOut left for Team X now known as Fnatic. MortaL then again stepped in for his team as Regaltos along with Clutchgod joined for the PMCO fall split 2019. The team again performed exceptionally well as the team again got qualified for the Global Finals with #2 position. Again the team secured #12 spot in the Globals with an amazing chicken dinner. Then clutchgod left for his original team, team 8bit, as Aman and Sangwan joined SouL. The team then got invited for the Peace Keepers Elite Championship, where they again finished #12. Then the team played PMPL as they were directly qualified from PMCO fall. There the team secured #8 to advance its way to regional finals. But unfortunately due to a string of bad performances, the team finished #13 and was their first time that they didn't qualify for the globals. Currently the team lives in their gaming house in Mumbai along with team 8bit and they are collaboratively known as Team S8UL.
